About me

As I was growing up, I was split between two worlds. I was always good at math and science, but also grew to love theater and playing music. I went to college as a double major in theater and chemistry, and eventually graduated with a music degree. Music took me to Austin TX where I played in bands and was able to tour around the world with my band Loves It. That project slowed down in 2014 and I came to Nashville looking for something new - I wanted to learn something to marry the creative and analytical sides of my brain, and this is how I came to find Nashville Software School. My favorite part of learning software development is what is also my favorite part of making music - dreaming up something new that did not exist before, figuring out how to piece it togther, creating it, and then presenting a new tiny invention to the world. I am stoked to always continue to learn new technologies and continue to grow and challenge myself creatively and intellectually. Also, when I'm not crushing code, I'm usually cooking tasty vegetables, tending to my increasingly feral garden, picking guitar or out dancing to classic country bands.

VWAY

A vegan restaurant finder app

VWAY is a CRUD app designed to allow users to search for and save vegan friendly restaurants in the United States. Once added to favorites, the restaurant can be commented on, emailed, or deleted from favorites. VWAY uses angular.js for the front end framework, firebase for user storage, and a node.js proxy server for handling the API calls to factual and google places.

Deployed link: https://www.vway.us
